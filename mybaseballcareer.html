My Baseball Career

Thanks to my father, I began to play baseball at the age of 3. Ever since, it has been the thing I like to do most. At the age of 18, I played on Team Quebec during the summer. From August 2018 to May 2019, I attended Chesapeke College in Wye Mills, Maryland, in order to play collegiate baseball. Today, I am lucky enough to play Divison 1 baseball for the UMaine Black Bears!
